# Customer Behavior and Sales Analysis (SQL Project)
## Project Overvieww
This project analyzes customer behavior and sales trends using a retail shopping dataset. The goal is to answer real business questions using SQL and generate actionable insights.
## Project Objectives
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior using SQL
* Identify key drivers of revenue and sales performance
* Evaluate the effectiveness of discounts on customer spending
* Determine top performing products and categories
* Generate business actionable insights to support decision making
## Business Questions
* What drives revenue?
* Which products perform best?
* Do discounts increase spending?
* Who are the most valuable customers?
## Tools and Skills Used
* MYSQL (Joins, GROUP BY, CASE, Aggregations)
* Data Cleaning
* Exploratory Data Analysis
## Dataset Information
The dataset used in this project was gotten through a professional network for strictly analytical and educational purposes. The dataset includes;
* Customer demographics (Age, etc)
* Product categories and items
* Sales and revenue data
* Payment methods and shipping types
## Key Insights
* Total revenue: **233K+** from **3,900 customers**
* Clothing is the top revenue generating category (**$104K**)
* Blouses are the most purchased product
* Customers aged **45+** generate the highest revenue
* Credit cards generate the most revenue
* Discounts do not significantly increase purchase value
* Free shipping is the most preferred option
## Key Takeaways
Customer behavior is influenced more by:
* Convenience (Shipping)
* Demographics (age)
* Product demand
... rather than discounts alone
## Business Recommendations
* Focus on high performing categories like clothing
* Retain high value and repeat customers
* Use discount strategically not excessively
* Target peak seasons (e.g., Fall campaigns)
* Offer conditional free shipping to boost order value
